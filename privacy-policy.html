隐私政策

Privacy Policy · 最后更新：2026 年 2 月

简短版本：你的个人记录数据 100% 存储在你的设备上，不会上传到任何服务器。本应用仅通过网络获取文章内容更新，不传输任何个人数据。

概述

「导了吗」（DaoLeMa，以下简称"本应用"）非常重视你的隐私。本隐私政策说明了我们如何处理你在使用本应用时产生的数据。

1. 个人数据

本应用不收集任何个人身份信息（PII）。你在应用中记录的所有个人数据，包括但不限于：

  • 行为记录（时间、触发原因、情绪、感受等）
  • 设置偏好（密码、提醒等）
  • AI 分析报告

均仅存储在你的 iPhone 本地存储中，绝不会被传输到任何远程服务器。我们在技术上无法获取这些数据。

2. 网络请求

本应用会通过网络请求获取以下内容：

  • 文章内容更新 — 从我们的静态服务器获取最新的科学文章列表。此请求不携带任何用户标识、设备信息或个人数据，仅下载文章的 JSON 数据。获取后的文章会缓存在设备本地，支持离线阅读。

除此之外，本应用不会发起任何网络请求。本应用不包含广告、不包含应用内购买，所有功能均已完整包含。

3. 数据存储

所有个人数据使用 Apple 的 SwiftData 框架存储在设备本地。数据随你的 iPhone 备份（iCloud 备份或电脑备份）一同保存。我们无法访问这些备份中的数据。

4. 第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。我们不使用 Firebase、Google Analytics、Facebook SDK 或任何类似的用户追踪工具。我们从设计上就选择了不收集用户数据。

5. AI 分析功能

应用提供数据导出功能，你可以选择将数据复制到第三方 AI 工具进行分析。这一操作完全由你主动发起和控制，我们不会自动将数据发送给任何 AI 服务。使用第三方 AI 工具时，请参阅该工具的隐私政策。

6. 数据安全

本应用支持密码锁和 Face ID / Touch ID 保护。密码以 SHA-256 哈希形式存储，原始密码不会被明文保存。进入后台时，应用会自动显示隐私遮罩，防止多任务视图中泄露内容。

7. 儿童隐私

本应用面向 17 岁及以上用户，不面向儿童提供服务。

8. 隐私政策更新

如果我们更新此隐私政策，将通过应用更新通知你。继续使用本应用即表示你同意更新后的政策。

9. 联系我们

如果你对此隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过以下邮箱联系我们：

contact@daolema.app

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

In short: All your personal data is stored 100% on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server. The app only fetches article content updates from the internet — no personal data is transmitted.

Overview

DaoLeMa ("the App") takes your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we handle data generated during your use of the App.

1. Personal Data

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). All personal data you record in the App, including but not limited to:

  • Activity records (time, triggers, mood, feelings, etc.)
  • Settings preferences (passcode, reminders, etc.)
  • AI analysis reports

are stored exclusively on your iPhone's local storage and will never be transmitted to any remote server. We have no technical ability to access this data.

2. Network Requests

The App makes network requests only for:

  • Article content updates — Fetches the latest science articles from our static server. This request does not carry any user identifiers, device information, or personal data. It only downloads article JSON data, which is then cached locally for offline reading.

Beyond this, the App does not make any network requests. The App contains no ads, no in-app purchases, and all features are fully included.

3. Data Storage

All personal data is stored locally on the device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Data is included in your iPhone backups (iCloud or computer backups). We cannot access data in these backups.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not use Firebase, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any similar user tracking tools. We chose by design not to collect user data.

5. AI Analysis Feature

The App provides a data export feature, allowing you to copy your data to third-party AI tools for analysis. This action is entirely initiated and controlled by you. We never automatically send data to any AI service. When using third-party AI tools, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Security

The App supports passcode lock and Face ID / Touch ID protection. Passcodes are stored as SHA-256 hashes; plain-text passwords are never saved. When entering the background, the App automatically displays a privacy overlay to prevent content exposure in the multitasking view.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 17 and above. It is not designed for or marketed to children.

8. Policy Updates

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify you through an app update. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

contact@daolema.app
